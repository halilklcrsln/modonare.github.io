## Introduction

Welcome to MODONARE ("we," "our," or "us"). MODONARE is an astro-acoustic wellness application that provides personalized music experiences based on your astrological birth chart. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you set up your MODONARE profile during the onboarding process, you provide us with the following information:

**Identity Information:**
- Full name
- Date of birth
- Time of birth
- Place of birth (country and city)
- Gender
- Profile photograph (optional)

**Account Preferences:**
- Selected application language
- Selected application theme (Dark / Light)

**Subscription Information:**
- When you purchase a subscription, payment is processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not collect, store, or process your payment card details. We only receive a confirmation of your subscription status from the platform.

### 1.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be explicit about what we do **not** collect:

- We do not collect your device's GPS location or real-time location data
- We do not collect browsing history
- We do not collect contacts
- We do not collect data from other applications on your device
- We do not collect biometric data beyond what you voluntarily provide
- We do not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics SDKs
- We do not collect data from minors under the age of 17

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 Core Purpose: Birth Chart & Astrological Calculations

The primary purpose of collecting your birth date, birth time, and place of birth is to calculate your **natal birth chart**, including:

- Your **Sun Sign** (zodiac sign) — determined by your birth date
- Your **Rising Sign (Ascendant)** — determined by your birth date, exact birth time, and geographic coordinates of your birth location
- Your **natal chart visualization** — a graphical representation of planetary positions at the time of your birth

These calculations are performed **entirely on your device** using embedded astronomical libraries. Your birth data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party for the purpose of astrological calculations.

### 2.2 Music Personalization

Your Sun Sign and Rising Sign are used to:
- Determine which personalized music compositions (e.g., LUMINA for Aries, TERRA for Taurus) are presented to you in the My Day section
- Determine which music files are downloaded to your device via On-Demand Resources
- Display your personalized Astro-Acoustic Insight content in the Info screen

### 2.3 Application Functionality

Your profile information (name, gender, profile photo) is used solely to:
- Personalize the in-app experience (e.g., displaying your name in greetings)
- Display your profile in the Profile screen
- Show gender-appropriate astrological personality descriptions

### 2.4 Subscription Management

Your subscription status (free or premium) is used to:
- Unlock or restrict access to premium content (all zodiac music, full Sound Lab library, full ambient sound library)
- Display appropriate content based on your subscription tier

## 3. Data Storage & Security

### 3.1 Local Storage Only

**All personal data you provide — including your name, birth information, profile photo, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's Core Data framework (iOS) or Room Database (Android).**

We do not operate servers that store your personal information. We do not have databases containing your birth data, name, or any other personal information you enter into MODONARE.

### 3.2 What This Means for You

- If you delete the MODONARE application from your device, all your data is permanently deleted
- If you use MODONARE on a new device, you will need to re-enter your information as it cannot be transferred from our side (we don't have it)
- We cannot recover your data if your device is lost or reset
- We do not offer cloud sync or backup of your personal data

### 3.3 Profile Photograph

Your profile photograph, if provided, is:
- Stored locally on your device only
- Never uploaded to any server
- Never transmitted over any network
- Used solely for display within the application

### 3.4 Security Measures

Although your data is stored locally, we implement the following security practices:
- Data is stored within the application's sandboxed storage, inaccessible to other applications
- The application does not request unnecessary system permissions
- Network communications (solely for subscription verification with Apple/Google) use TLS encryption

## 4. Audio Content & Downloads

### 4.1 On-Demand Music Downloads

MODONARE uses Apple's On-Demand Resources (iOS) system to download music files to your device. These downloads:
- Are triggered by your zodiac sign profile and subscription status
- Are stored locally on your device for offline playback
- Are managed by the operating system and may be cleared by the OS if storage space is needed
- Do not contain any personal data

### 4.2 Audio Files

All music and ambient sound files in MODONARE:
- Are pre-produced compositions; they do not record or analyze your listening patterns for transmission
- Are played locally using AVFoundation (iOS) / ExoPlayer (Android)
- Your listening history and playback habits are not collected or transmitted

## 5. Third-Party Services

### 5.1 Apple App Store / Google Play (Subscription Processing)

When you purchase a subscription, you interact directly with Apple's App Store or Google Play. These platforms process your payment and provide MODONARE with a subscription entitlement token. We receive only:
- Whether you have an active subscription (yes/no)
- The type of subscription (Weekly / Monthly / Annual)
- The subscription expiry date

We do not receive your payment details, credit card information, or billing address.

Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/  
Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 5.2 Astronomical Calculation Library

MODONARE uses the Kerykeion astronomical library (or equivalent embedded library) for birth chart calculations. This library operates entirely on-device and does not transmit any data externally.

### 5.3 No Advertising Networks

MODONARE does not integrate any advertising SDKs, ad networks, or demand-side platforms. Premium subscribers and free users are never shown advertisements generated by third-party networks.

### 5.4 No Analytics SDKs

MODONARE does not integrate third-party analytics platforms (such as Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, Crashlytics, etc.) that would collect behavioral data about your usage.

## 7. Your Rights & Choices

### 7.1 Access & Correction

Since all your data is stored on your own device, you have full access to it at all times. You can:
- View your profile information in the Profile screen at any time
- Edit your profile information by tapping "Edit Details" in the Profile screen
- Update your birth information, which will trigger a recalculation of your birth chart

### 7.2 Deletion

You can delete all your personal data in two ways:

**Option A — Delete Account (within the app):**
- Navigate to Profile → Settings → "Delete Account"
- Confirm deletion in the dialog
- All Core Data (iOS) / Room Database (Android) entries are permanently erased
- Your subscription is not automatically cancelled; you must cancel it separately through the App Store / Google Play

**Option B — Uninstall the application:**
- Deleting MODONARE from your device removes all locally stored data

### 7.3 Subscription Cancellation

To cancel your subscription:
- **iOS:** Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → MODONARE → Cancel
- **Android:** Google Play → Subscriptions → MODONARE → Cancel

Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

### 7.4 Language & Theme Preferences

You can change your language and theme preferences at any time:
- Navigate to Profile → Settings
- Select your preferred language or toggle your preferred theme

### 7.5 Profile Photograph

You can remove your profile photograph at any time by:
- Navigating to Profile → Edit Details
- Selecting the option to remove the photo

## 9. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the following provisions apply:

### 9.1 Legal Basis for Processing

We process the personal information you provide on the following legal bases:
- **Contractual necessity:** To provide you with the core service (birth chart calculation, personalized music)
- **Legitimate interest:** To improve application stability and user experience
- **Consent:** For optional features such as profile photograph

### 9.2 Your GDPR Rights

You have the following rights under GDPR:
- **Right of Access:** You can access all your data directly within the application
- **Right to Rectification:** You can correct your data via Profile → Edit Details
- **Right to Erasure:** You can delete all data via Profile → Delete Account or by uninstalling the app
- **Right to Data Portability:** As all data is local, you have direct access to it at all times
- **Right to Restrict Processing:** You can stop all processing by deleting the application
- **Right to Object:** You can object to processing by contacting us at the email address below

Since data is processed locally on your device, many GDPR obligations (data breach notification, data processor agreements) are limited in their application to MODONARE's architecture. We are committed to the spirit of GDPR regardless.
